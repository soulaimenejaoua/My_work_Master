Use the app in 3 simple steps every day:

  1. Check Planner for today’s events and deadlines.
  2. Move tasks on the Board from Planned to In Progress when you start working on them.
  3. Move finished tasks to Completed and prepare tomorrow’s tasks before you leave.

Tip: keep no more than 3 tasks in progress at the same time.
